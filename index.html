Who we are

Care that feels like family, because that's how we built it

Pious Health Care Services is a Maryland home care agency founded on a simple belief: people heal and live best in the comfort of their own homes, surrounded by the routines and the people they know. We pair experienced nurses, aides, and homemakers with the families we serve, and we stay involved for as long as care is needed.

Whether your family needs a few hours of help around the house each week or ongoing skilled nursing support, our team builds a plan around your loved one's needs — not the other way around. Every caregiver we send into a home is there to support dignity, routine, and peace of mind.

What we do

Five ways we help families stay home, together

Skilled Nursing

Licensed nurses provide hands-on medical care at home — medication management, wound care, and health monitoring, coordinated closely with your doctors.

Home Health Aide

Trained aides help with bathing, dressing, and mobility — the daily routines that keep your loved one safe, clean, and comfortable.

Personal Care

One-on-one support with the personal tasks of daily living, delivered with patience and respect for privacy and dignity.

Homemaker Services

Light housekeeping, meal preparation, laundry, and errands — so home stays a place of comfort rather than a source of stress.

Palliative Support

Compassionate, comfort-focused care for individuals managing serious illness, centered on quality of life for the whole family.
